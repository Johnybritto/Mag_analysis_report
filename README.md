# Mag Analysis Report

Repository for saving business magazine analysis reports so insights are not lost across chats.

## Saved Format: Mag Analysis

**Mag Analysis** is the standard structure for analyzing business magazines with a sharp business, investor, portfolio, and career lens.

### 1. Issue Snapshot
Short 5–7 line overview of the whole magazine issue.

### 2. Article Map
Major articles/topics covered and why they matter.

### 3. THEMES & COVERAGE
Includes:
- The Core Thesis
- Macro Drivers & Timeliness
- The Contrarian Take

### 4. DATA & CLAIMS
Includes:
- Hard Metrics & Evidence
- Projections vs. Opinions

### 5. BUSINESS IMPLICATIONS
Includes:
- Strategy & Execution
- Roadblocks & Pivots
- Winners vs. Losers

### 6. RELEVANCE & FRAMEWORK
Includes:
- Universal Takeaways
- Editorial Framing
- Rabbit Holes / Deeper Research Areas

### 7. Signal vs Noise
Separates real insights from magazine filler.

### 8. Theme → Sector → Stock Mapping
Connects magazine themes to sectors and listed companies.

### 9. Portfolio Relevance for You
Checks whether the issue affects existing holdings and watchlist stocks.

### 10. DevOps / Career Relevance for You
Extracts career and skill relevance for a DevOps / Platform Engineering background.

### 11. SO WHAT
Includes:
- The Big Idea
- The 60-Second Briefing
- Action Items
- Stocks that can be tracked and invested

### 12. Phase 3 Candidates
Shortlist of stocks worth deeper analysis before any buy decision.

## Explicit Rule
Do **not** include an Investment Readiness Score section. The report should identify what is worth tracking and why; Phase 3 decides whether a stock is investable at current price.
